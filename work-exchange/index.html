Work Exchange Membership Program

Ruth Obenchain Recreation Center

RORC offers a limited Work Exchange Membership Program for eligible individuals or families.

How It Works

Approved participants complete assigned facility duties in exchange for membership access. A maximum of five (5) Work Exchange memberships are available at any time.

Program Requirements

Participants are required to complete at least one (1) hour of approved work per week. Work Exchange memberships are reviewed every 90 days and are subject to management approval.

Apply

To inquire about availability or apply, please contact RORC management.
